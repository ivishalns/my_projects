Model Agnostic

Your cloud. Your models. Your rules.

Enterprise AI vendor lock-in is a massive problem. Banks on Azure want GPT. Healthcare on GCP wants Gemini. Government clients need on-prem open source. A platform locked to one provider disqualifies itself from half the market. Pockets is built to work with all of them — simultaneously.

STRATEGY A

Client Choice

Enterprise picks their LLM based on existing cloud agreements and data residency. The adapter layer translates — sector and client layers stay completely model-neutral.

STRATEGY B

Intelligent Routing

The platform selects the optimal model per task. Complex regulatory reasoning routes to the most capable. Simple lookups go to faster, cheaper alternatives. Cost and quality balanced automatically.

STRATEGY C

Resilience

No single provider dependency. If the primary model is unavailable, automatic failover routes to a secondary without disruption. Performance monitoring runs continuously across all providers.

PROMPT ASSEMBLY FLOW

This entire flow executes in seconds. What would take a compliance analyst days of manual research happens in a single query.

01Request

A compliance officer submits a natural language query — "Are we compliant with the new OCC guidance on third-party risk management?"

02Context

The engine resolves who's asking: their institution's config, applicable sector pack, user role, permission level. Prior interactions inform context.

03Assemble

Three layers merge into one prompt: base reasoning (obligation extraction, gap analysis, citation logic) + sector knowledge (OCC guidance, FFIEC frameworks) + client context (their vendor list, prior findings).

04Route

The orchestration engine selects the optimal LLM — client's preferred model (Strategy A), best for regulatory reasoning (Strategy B), or available fallback (Strategy C).

05Adapt

The assembled prompt is formatted for the target model's specific API, context window limits, and prompting patterns. Model-specific reasoning variants activate.

06Respond

Output includes cited regulatory sources (specific OCC bulletin sections), COMPLIANT/GAP/MISSING scoring, severity-ranked findings, and remediation recommendations — formatted for the user's role.

07Learn

Interaction data flows back: reasoning effectiveness to the base layer, new regulatory references to the sector pack, usage patterns to the client config. The platform improves with every query.
